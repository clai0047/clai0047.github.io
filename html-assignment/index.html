About me

This is my 1st time using HTML. During my nearly 28 years, I have picked up several creative hobbies I enjoy. This includes but is not limited to sewing, embroidery, macrame, visual art, playing piano/ music and photography. I also have a passion for plants and nature in general. Caring for plants around my house is one of my favourite activities to decompress.

I deal with chronic pain on a daily basis and have for many years, I consider myself extremly resilient. I've learned to adapt and find ways to go about life that work for me. Over the years, that looked different. I used to navigate life using a service dog, she would go to doctors' appointments, grocery shopping, anywhere I needed to be she would be there and would alert me to abnormal heart rates and blood pressure levels. This was so I could safely sit down and avoid passing out. I'm now able to take a blood pressure medication that allows me to manage my symptoms on my own. This gave me a significant amount of freedom. I still have my dog, Poppy, who is 8 now. She remains a wonderful addition to my life and we still have an amazing bond.

  • Macrame
  • Visual Art
